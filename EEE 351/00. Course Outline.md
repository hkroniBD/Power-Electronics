## **EEE 351 – Industrial and Power Electronics (3.00 Credits)**

### **Lecture-wise Course Outline**

| **Module**             | **Lecture No.** | **Topic**                         | **Key Contents**                                  | **Physical / Industrial Significance**                              |
| ---------------------- | --------------- | --------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Fundamentals**       | 1               | Introduction to Power Electronics | Definition, energy conversion, AC → DC → AC chain | Explains how electrical energy is shaped and controlled in machines |
| **Power Devices**      | 2               | BJT, MOSFET, IGBT                 | Structure, switching, losses                      | These devices act as electronic power switches                      |
|                        | 3               | Thyristors (SCR)                  | Triggering, commutation, ratings                  | Used in high-power rectifiers and heating                           |
| **AC → DC**            | 4               | Uncontrolled Rectifiers           | 1-phase & 3-phase diode rectifiers                | Convert AC to fixed DC                                              |
|                        | 5               | Controlled Rectifiers             | SCR firing angle control                          | Variable DC voltage for DC drives                                   |
|                        | 6               | Dual Converter                    | Reversible power flow                             | Used in regenerative motor drives                                   |
| **AC → AC**            | 7               | Phase Controlled AC Converters    | 1-phase & 3-phase voltage controllers             | Speed control of motors and heaters                                 |
|                        | 8               | AC Switch & Cycloconverter        | AC chopping, low-frequency AC                     | Used in large motor drives                                          |
| **DC → DC**            | 9               | Choppers                          | Step-down and step-up                             | DC voltage control                                                  |
|                        | 10              | Switching Regulators              | Buck, Boost, Buck-Boost                           | Used in power supplies and chargers                                 |
| **DC → AC**            | 11              | Single Phase Inverter             | PWM, square wave                                  | UPS, solar inverter                                                 |
|                        | 12              | Three Phase Inverter              | VSI, PWM control                                  | Industrial motor drives                                             |
| **Applications**       | 13              | Converter Applications            | Drives, EVs, solar, welding                       | Shows how theory meets real industry                                |
| **Industrial Heating** | 14              | Electrical Heating                | Resistance, induction, dielectric, arc            | Used in steel, melting, drying                                      |
| **PLC**                | 15              | PLC Introduction                  | Automation, relay replacement                     | Brain of industrial automation                                      |
|                        | 16              | PLC Hardware                      | CPU, memory, I/O                                  | Physical architecture of PLC                                        |
|                        | 17              | PLC Programming                   | Ladder logic, timers, counters                    | Logic control of machines                                           |
|                        | 18              | Testing & Commercial PLC          | Debugging, Siemens, Allen-Bradley                 | Real-world industrial deployment                                    |
