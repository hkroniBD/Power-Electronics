## ğŸ’¡ Key Design Insights

- **Transformer Derating:** The capacitor-input filter causes high peak currents, necessitating a transformer with a higher current rating (e.g., 10A) than the DC load current (5A).
- **Voltage Margins:** Using diodes with a PIV rating much higher than the theoretical minimum (600V vs 163V) ensures reliability against voltage spikes and transients.
